The Future of Iot Devices

One click of a button can show the advancements of technology

The future of IoT devices is inevitable as technology continues to adapt and reach new heights. From what we have learned in the past, we as people have evolved and so has our creations and innovations. The internet and other technology started as low-memory pieces of equipment that only used for communication with other human beings. How it sits today, some of our IoT devices are now smaller than our very own hands. But it comes at a cost with IoT devices being a double-edged sword. Behind its greatness lies vast amount of issues that cause catastrophic harm with just the click of a button. Here is a short list of advantages and disadvantages regarding the future of IoT devices.

  • Economical beneftis
  • Efficiency
  • Stronger IoT regulations and laws
  • Security improvement
  • Loss of jobs
  • Complexitiy
  • Overdependance
  • Increased security risks

Optimism is considered a valuable asset when discussing technology. Humans want life to become further accessible through the use of technology, and IoT is just one example. With a click of a button or the sound of someone's voice tasks have become easier which frees up our hands. On the other side of this discussion, is alertness. If society does not remain vigilant on the topic of technology, turmoil may occur. It could possibly end businesses where human workers are now being replaced by IoT devices or having confidential information become exposed which places the victims in danger on the internet and in the physical world. What may cause even further disorganization between society and technology is people losing a piece of their humanity to try and fit into a future technological utopian society.
