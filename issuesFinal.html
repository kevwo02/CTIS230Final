Issues with IoT Devices

There is a vast amount of issues when regarding technology. Technology has caused issue to physical health by straining eyesight and reduced movement or mental health issues through the use of online bullying which causes emotional concern to self and others. For IoT devices, these are more or less the same. Though these issues may impact our use of these devices, the problems lie further deeper inside of its technology. Two concerning issues of discussion are difficulty in technicality and vulnerabilities.

Difficulties in Technicality

An Internet of Things (IoT) device is a physical device that collects and exchanges data with other devices through the use of the internet. These devices come in various sizes and equipment with different purposes. Through the advance of technology, IoT devices have added towards a significant step in digital intellignce as it can update real-time data with no human interaction needed to give the device a command or an action.
