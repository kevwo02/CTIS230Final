IoT World Today is a website solely focused on IoT devices. The website breaks down to different fields of areas where IoT devices are being used. It also gives a variety of other content related to IoT devices and the future of autonomy.

The Hacker News

The Hacker News (THN) is a website that predominantly cover topics related to cybersecurity news and IoT. The website's news cover current events and possible future trends to inform their readers and make predictions of what will happen next.

Wired is a news publishing company that focuses are various topics. One of their main topics is technology, and one of their subsections talk about IoT. The section covers any kind of new related to IoT devices and further connects them to other news topics that show similarities.
