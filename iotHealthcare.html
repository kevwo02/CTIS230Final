Example of Iot Devices Used in Healthcare

Click on the image for further information.

In the healthcare field, medicine is crucial. Medicine keeps people healthy, prevents diseases, and reduces any long-term issues. Technology and medicine are highly associated with one another, and developing IoT devices will continue to ease the field. IoT devices have helped further communication between doctors, patients, and everyone else in between. As well as greatly enhancing the data that is being input, used, and collected.

Smart Thermometers Examples:

Kinsa Smart Thermometer, iHealth Smart Thermometer, VAVA Smart Baby Thermometer, Braun ThermoScan 7

Smart Watch Examples:

Apple Watch, Samsung Galaxy Watch, Fossil Smartwatch, IWO Smartwatch

Smart First Responding Vehicles and Appliances:

Smart Ambulance, Red Ninja's Life First Emergency Traffic Control (LiFE), Hamad Medical Command and Control Vehicles

Adavacments in X-Ray Technology:

Society has either experienced or seen media where films of X-Ray displayed behind a white background. Now, X-rays are considered as an IoT device. After the images are taken, they are then stored and uploaded to the patient's medical records which can then be seen by doctors, patients, and even Artificial Intelligence (AI). This advancement lowers wait time, is cost efficient, and has the potential to save lives.
