Example of Iot Devices Used in Agriculture

Plant Sensors

There are several variations of plant sensors for different purposes. Some are used for measuring water levels, or tracking fertilization, and can even be used to monitor pest infestations. These IoT devices may seem miniature but they are considered vital to humanity.

Smart Tractors

One of the neccesities to human survival is food. Yet there is a lack of resources that provides such necessity. Smart tractors are autonomous tractors capable of relieving issues that has caused a downfall to agriculture. These tractors include sensors, WiFi and Bluetooth connectivity, real-time analytical management, and more.

Smart Weather Stations

An important factor to agriculture is weather. Smart weather stations uses sensors to report the data that is being tracked and recorded to a database. This vital information allow farm workers to monitor weather patterns and reactionary time to maintain healthy agriculture and livestock.
